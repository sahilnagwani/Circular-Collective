# **App Name**: Circular Collective

## Core Features:

- Curated Product Catalog: Browse a dynamic product grid of vintage, pre-loved, and upcycled luxury items, with advanced filtering options (condition, material) and real-time search functionality.
- Detailed Product View & Quick Preview: View comprehensive product details, high-resolution imagery, and utilize a 'Quick View' modal for fast previews without navigating away from the shop page.
- AI-Enhanced Product Storytelling: Leverage a generative AI tool to craft unique, compelling narratives and highlight the sustainability journey and special attributes for each luxury item.
- Seamless Shopping Cart Management: Add, remove, and update quantities of items in a persistently stored shopping cart, with real-time updates and a cart item counter.
- Personalized Wishlist Functionality: Users can save favorite items to a personal wishlist for later consideration, with persistent storage of selections.
- Streamlined Checkout Process: A multi-step checkout including customer information forms, order summary, dynamic shipping calculation, and payment simulation.
- Dynamic Environmental Impact Display: Visually showcase the calculated environmental savings (water saved, CO₂ emissions reduced) associated with each product and total purchases, emphasizing the circular economy.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen color palette creates a dark luxury aesthetic. The background color is a deep, desaturated grey (#19191A), providing a sophisticated canvas. The primary accent color, a muted violet (#6A6080), offers subtle elegance for interactive elements. A brighter blue (#7D89DB) serves as the accent color for key calls-to-action, ensuring visual distinction and modernity.
- Headline font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) for an editorial luxury feel. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for modern minimal readability. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Minimalistic and clean vector icons are used throughout the platform, enhancing navigation and product features without distracting from the luxury aesthetic.
- The layout features a modern, minimal luxury interface with generous whitespace. Large editorial imagery dominates key sections, employing a centered logo navigation, dynamic product grids, and a full-screen mega menu with smooth transitions.
- Smooth motion design and micro-interactions are integral, including GSAP-powered hero text reveals, 3D parallax effects, scroll reveal animations, and subtle hover effects on products and navigation items, creating a premium browsing experience.
